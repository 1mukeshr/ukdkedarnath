About Us

UKD Kedarnath — Yuva Uttarakhand. Our mission and commitment to the hills.

Youth wing of UKD • Active in all 13 districts

Who We Are

We are the youth wing of Uttarakhand Kranti Dal (UKD), rooted in Kedarnath and the hills. Our aim is to give voice to the youth and strength to the mountains—through organisation, leadership, and collective action across Uttarakhand.

Our Mission

To empower Uttarakhand's youth through organisation, leadership, and collective action for the development of the state.

Our Vision

A strong, united Uttarakhand where every young person has the opportunity to lead and contribute.

What We Do

  • Youth organisation — Building a strong, active youth base at state, district, block, and booth levels.
  • Leadership & training — Identifying and training young leaders to take up responsibility in the organisation and in society.
  • Grassroots connect — Engaging with local issues, culture, and development so that youth perspectives are heard.
  • State & hills — Advocating for Uttarakhand’s interests: employment, education, environment, and equitable development for the hills.
